Biography



I am a Hungarian tenor with a lifelong passion for singing, having begun my musical journey in primary and secondary school choirs. Currently, I am pursuing my studies at TU Dublin.

My stage experience includes performances at the National Theatre of Miskolc, where I appeared in productions such as Don Carlos and Shakespeare in Love. I have also been a returning performer at the Miskolc Opera Festival, taking part in works like Don Giovanni and Cavalleria Rusticana. Beyond opera, I was a member of a vocal quartet that achieved remarkable success, earning gold medals in both of its competitions. My artistic path reflects dedication, versatility, and a deep love for performance.

Currently, in my first year, I am exploring the nuances of performance art, classical singing, and stage presence.
